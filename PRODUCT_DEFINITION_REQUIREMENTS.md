# Product Definition Requirements (PDR)

## 1. Product Overview

### Product Name (Working)

FieldR (Evolved) / Community Sports Platform (Name TBD)

### Vision

Enable people in Saudi Arabia to easily **find, organize, and sustain participation in sports** through a single platform that handles discovery, organization, payments, and basic wellbeing support — inclusive of gender preferences and cultural norms.

### Mission

Reduce friction to play sports by providing **community discovery, tournament organization, payments, and safety-first participation tools** for amateur and semi‑organized sports.

### Strategic Alignment (Saudi Vision 2030)

* Increase sports participation across genders
* Enable community & amateur sports ecosystems
* Support workforce wellbeing and active lifestyles
* Encourage private-sector sports events & tournaments

---

## 2. Target Users & Personas

### Primary Users

1. **Casual Players** – professionals, students, expats & Saudis seeking regular play
2. **Team Captains / Organizers** – manage games, teams, tournaments
3. **Tournament Organizers** – community leaders, academies, corporates

### Secondary Users

4. **Venues / Grounds** – want bookings & visibility
5. **Corporate HR / Wellness Teams** (future)

---

## 3. Core Problems Being Solved

1. Hard to find people to play preferred sports
2. Difficulty ensuring gender-appropriate / safe play environments
3. Poor organization of amateur tournaments
4. Manual, trust-based payment collection
5. No simple way to track participation, injuries, or recovery
6. Fragmented communication and scheduling
7. Limited promotion channels for community sports events

---

## 4. Product Capabilities (Full Scope)

### A. Sports Discovery & Community

* User profiles (sport preferences, skill level)
* Sport-based discovery (football, cricket, padel, etc.)
* Gender filters: men-only, women-only, unisex
* Location-based discovery

### B. Game & Tournament Organization

* Create games / events
* Create tournaments (knockout, league, groups)
* Player registration & approvals
* Team assignment

### C. Payments & Collections

* Player participation fees
* Tournament entry fees
* Optional escrow-style collection
* Refund & cancellation rules

### D. Scheduling & Communication

* Match schedules
* Push & in-app notifications
* Group chats / announcements

### E. Basic Player Performance Tracking

* Matches played
* Minutes played / attendance
* Basic stats (sport-dependent)

### F. Injury Tracking

* Injury logging (self-reported)
* Injury history
* Time-off from play

### G. Injury Recovery Planner

* Non-medical recovery timelines
* Return-to-play reminders
* Load management nudges

### H. Event Promotion

* Featured events
* Sponsored tournaments
* Venue promotions

---

## 5. Non-Goals (Explicitly Out of Scope)

* Elite athlete analytics
* Medical diagnosis or treatment
* Wearable hardware integration (early stages)
* Professional league management

---

## 6. Monetisation Model

### Primary Revenue

1. Transaction fee on payments
2. Tournament creation / management fees
3. Organizer subscriptions

### Secondary Revenue (Later)

4. Event promotion fees
5. Venue listing fees
6. Corporate league packages

---

## 7. MVP Scope (Investor-Ready)

### MUST HAVE

* User onboarding & profiles
* Sport discovery (limited sports)
* Gender-based access filters
* Create & join games
* Basic payment collection (no escrow)
* Scheduling & notifications

### MUST NOT HAVE

* Injury recovery planning
* Advanced tournaments
* Performance analytics
* Promotions

### MVP Success Metrics

* Monthly active players
* Games created per week
* Payment success rate

---

## 8. V1 Scope (Monetisation-Ready)

### ADDITIONS

* Tournament creation & management
* Payment enhancements (refunds, partial payments)
* Organizer dashboards
* Basic performance stats
* In-app communication

### V1 Success Metrics

* Paid tournaments created
* Revenue per organizer
* Retention of players

---

## 9. V2 Scope (Strategic Expansion)

### ADDITIONS

* Injury tracking & recovery planner
* Event promotion & sponsorships
* Venue integrations
* Corporate leagues & wellness programs
* Multi-sport scalability

### V2 Success Metrics

* Corporate clients onboarded
* Avg revenue per event
* Player safety engagement

---

## 10. Key Risks & Mitigations

### Risk: Overbuilding early

Mitigation: Strict MVP scope control

### Risk: Weak sport adoption

Mitigation: Focus on 1–2 anchor sports initially

### Risk: Regulatory sensitivity (health)

Mitigation: Non-medical positioning

---

## 11. Why This Is Investable in Saudi Arabia

* Participation-first, not elite-first
* Gender-inclusive by design
* Payments & tournaments enable revenue
* Expandable across sports & demographics

---
