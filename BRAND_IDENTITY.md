## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Energetic**: Use action words (Play, Join, Score, Win)
- **Inclusive**: "We" language, community-focused
- **Simple**: Clear, no jargon
- **Encouraging**: Positive reinforcement

### Tone by Context

**Marketing**
- Exciting, aspirational
- "Find your next game in seconds"
- "Never miss a match again"

**App UI**
- Direct, helpful
- "5 slots left - join now!"
- "Your game starts in 2 hours"

**Errors**
- Empathetic, solution-focused
- "Game is full, but you can join the waitlist"
- "Something went wrong. Let's try again"

**Success**
- Celebratory but not over-the-top
- "You're in! See you on the field"
- "Game created successfully"

## Animation Principles

### Motion Duration
```
Fast:    150ms  - Hover, focus states
Normal:  250ms  - Transitions, reveals
Slow:    400ms  - Page transitions, modals
```

### Easing
```
Standard: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1)
Enter:    cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1)
Exit:     cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 1, 1)
```

### Key Animations
- **Button Click**: Scale 0.98, bounce back
- **Card Hover**: Lift (translateY -4px) + shadow
- **Page Enter**: Fade + slide up
- **Modal**: Scale from 0.95 to 1, fade in
- **Loading**: Pulse or skeleton shimmer
